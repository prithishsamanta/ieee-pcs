About Us

"The art of communication is the language of leadership."


We believe in making an individual a master of words, a master of their communication skills.
Our objective is to raise the morale of students and work towards creating a society of confident and ambitious people.
Starting out as a small initiative, today we are proud to be one of the best chapters in VIT, with hopes of growing and achieving even greater heights.

Current Events

Mission To Mars

IEEE-PCS, brings you a mind-boggling adventure to the farthest corner of the inner solar system, giving you a ride to the future to the year 2075. Mask yourself as one of the six famous personalities from the 12 countries present at the round table conference. Debate, discuss and derive a solution as you clash against like minds prepared to colonize Mars at all costs. Get a chance to become a philanthropist, a politician, or a smart scientist. Ardent doctors and environmentalists are also welcome to get aboard in this mission. Impress the panel judges with your debating skills. Convince them that you have got it to colonize Mars. Will you be able to face two rounds of heated discussion, or will you get knocked out in the very first round? There is only one way to find out. So gear up your imagination, oil up your debating skills, tighten your seat belts, sit back, and join us on Mission to Mars with IEEE - PCS, departing off on 30th August!

1300 Reasons Why

IEEE PCS brings back 1300 Reasons Why, an opportunity to use your binge watching skills of TV shows to decipher and brainstorm an exciting set of riddles. There are 2 rounds. Round 1 starts with your team getting 100 virtual coins to spend on various mini games to be played. The team with maximum points obtained from the mini games will proceed to Round 2, which is the "Riddle Hunt". This consists of a set of riddles to be decoded. The last 2 teams to crack the riddles in the shortest time will enter a face off round. The team who solves the given puzzle or crossword in the shortest amount of time will be declared as the winner!

Place-It

Dream of being placed in a dream or super dream company but you aren't sure how to get there? Ask our esteemed alumni yourself. With an interactive session with placed students, motivational speakers and much more, PlaceIt is an event that promises to leave you inspired, motivated and give you more clarity about the placement procedure. Place-It brings speakers across all branches so that every student's doubts can be addressed, at one place, on one stage. It's time to put your concerns to rest and work towards building a successful future. Who knows-it may be you up on stage next time?
